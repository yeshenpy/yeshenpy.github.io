About

I am a PhD student at the Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) Lab, Tianjin University (TJU), advised by Prof. Jianye Hao. I received my Bachelor's degree from Tianjin University as well. My research mainly focuses on reinforcement learning and evolutionary algorithms.

I firmly believe in reinforcement learning and regard reinforcement as the core of intelligence. I am currently committed to advancing the application of reinforcement learning in embodied intelligence.
